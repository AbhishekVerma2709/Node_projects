Following app mines through the tweets of your followers and tell the what is right time and day to tweet to get most the retweet or favorites.
It asks for your your username or user id and gets your last tweet.Now it mines through last tweet of your followers and if the last tweet of any of followers is within last week based on user's last tweet time,then it a canditate for figuring out the best time.Based on this it will tell you when is the best time two tweet.



App is build with Angular.js on the client side and node.js on the client side.


Angular.js controller 'controller.js'


Node.js app - 'server.js'
